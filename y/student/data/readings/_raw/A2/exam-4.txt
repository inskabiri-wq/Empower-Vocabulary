PASSAGE_TITLE: Types of Families

PASSAGE:
A family is a group of people that have a common ancestor. They usually live together in the same house. Although it is a fact that not all families are the same, we can categorize them into different types.

{gap:1} It consists of parents and their children living together. It can be a positive environment to raise children if there is love, emotional support, low stress, and a stable economic environment. There are three types of nuclear families. In the first type the father works and the mother cares for the children. {gap:2} In the third type of family, both parents work. This last type of family is the most common in the USA and Canada.

In an extended family, different family members such as grandparents, aunts, uncles and cousins live together. {gap:3} The extended family may live together for financial reasons. When more adults live together, they can all contribute to the household budget. Also, when people get old or sick, they sometimes live with their adult children or relatives. {gap:4} Some people return to their parents' home after they break up because they need emotional support and help with child care.

Recently, many people are having children later in life. They only have one child because they don't have enough time to have another one. These types of families are called single child families. {gap:5} But research doesn't support this. Some families have no children at all. The couples that are in these families choose not to have children because they want to have more free time. Other couples choose not to have children for financial reasons.

Families with only one parent are called single parent families. There are several causes of these types of families. The first reason is divorce. {gap:6} Additionally, some parents never get married in the first place.

When the husband or wife in a marriage brings children with them from a past marriage, this is called a blended or reconstituted family. This type of family is now more common because of the increase in divorces. For some children this kind of family can be good because there are two parents, instead of just one. {gap:7}

As the basis of all societies, families of all types are very important. This is true now and will be true in the future as well.
