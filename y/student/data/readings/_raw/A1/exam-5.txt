PASSAGE_TITLE: My Day

PASSAGE:
I'm a shop assistant in a big electronics shop. My day starts at half past six when I get up and take a shower. Then I have breakfast at quarter past seven and I leave home at quarter to eight. I walk to the bus stop and I wait for the bus. The shop opens at half past eight and I start work. I sell TVs, DVDs and music systems. I like my job. It's interesting and I talk to a lot of people. I have lunch at half past twelve and I leave work at half past five. I usually play football with friends for about an hour. I have dinner at seven o'clock and I watch TV or listen to CDs in the evening. I go to bed at eleven o'clock.

# TRUEFALSE
LABEL: Part A
INSTRUCTIONS: Choose True or False for the following sentences.
1. His day starts at half past six.
   ANSWER: T
2. He has a wash every morning.
   ANSWER: T
3. The shop opens at 8:15.
   ANSWER: F
4. He works in an electronics shop.
   ANSWER: T
5. He doesn't like his job.
   ANSWER: F
6. His job is interesting.
   ANSWER: T
7. He leaves work at 5:30.
   ANSWER: T
8. He visits his friends in the evening.
   ANSWER: F

# MCQ
LABEL: Part B
INSTRUCTIONS: Circle the correct answer.
1. He has breakfast __________.
   a) at work
   b) at home
   c) on the bus
   d) in a cafe
   ANSWER: b
2. He goes to work __________.
   a) on foot
   b) by car
   c) by bus
   d) by subway
   ANSWER: c
3. He goes to bed __________.
   a) before 10 o'clock
   b) before 12 o'clock
   c) at 10 o'clock
   d) at 12 o'clock
   ANSWER: b

# WRITING
LABEL: Part C
INSTRUCTIONS: Tell your friend about your daily routine.
