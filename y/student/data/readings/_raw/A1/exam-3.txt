PASSAGE:
Welcome to the home of Julia Hill! Julia is a young woman. *She* is from Seattle. *She* is 25 years old, and she has two brothers, aged 12 and 16. *They* live in Seattle with their parents but she lives in a large redwood tree in California. {gap:1} *Her* *home* is over 600 years old.

Julia is a member of Greenpeace and she wants to save trees and forests in the world because trees *produce* oxygen and they clean the air. They are very important for the future of Earth. {gap:2} However, a company called Axe Inc. wants to cut them down but the company can't cut down this tree because Julia lives in it!

What does Julia do every day? She gets up early. The mornings are cold, so she gets dressed fast. She combs her hair and washes her face. Then she eats her breakfast. After breakfast, Julia *checks* her mailbox and reads her mails. She *receives* a lot of letters every day. {gap:3} In the afternoon, she exercises. She goes jogging and climbs around in the tall tree. After that, she takes a shower and talks to many people on her cell phone. Sometimes people come and visit her and they bring fresh water, food, and clothes. Julia goes to sleep at 9:00 p.m. Julia lives in the tree for 738 days. Life in the tree is difficult. In winter, it snows, and it's very windy. Sometimes Julia is *scared*, because she lives alone in the tree but she doesn't want to give up her fight and come down.

Finally, the company says, "we aren't going to cut down the trees." Julia is ready to come down from the tree. {gap:4} She has a message for every man, woman, and child: Love the trees. Care for our earth.
