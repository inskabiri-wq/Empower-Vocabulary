PASSAGE:
The word "Bollywood" is a mix of the words "Bombay" (a city in India now called "Mumbai") and "Hollywood". Bollywood is the largest movie-making business in India (and the world). {gap:1} Every year, Bollywood makes more movies and money than Hollywood does.

Bollywood movies share certain features. First, most Bollywood films are a minimum of three hours long. {gap:2} The movie usually stops in the middle and people take a short break.

Also many Bollywood movies are about love. Often in a story, the boy's parents don't like the girl, or the girl's parents don't like the boy. The lovers, divided by their families, try to be together. {gap:3}

Another important part of Bollywood movies is music. Singing and dancing in a Bollywood film is very common. The music is often a mix of Indian pop songs and traditional melodies. {gap:4} Some people think the music is as important as the movie's story. In some Indian theaters, people watching the movie will sing and dance with the performers on screen!
