🎮 Pick a game mode

Two ways to run a classroom session — pick one.

🏁 Vocab Race · Setup

🦹 The Heist · Setup

Students answer vocab questions to earn coins, then race to crack each other's vault passwords using Taboo-style hints. Defaults are tuned for a 10-minute session with ~20 students.

After you create the room you'll move to the live host view (lobby → game → results).

🎧 Listening Exam · Setup

You'll play the audio on the classroom speakers — students see only the questions on their phones (no audio file, no transcript leaked). After they submit, they see only their raw score until you press Reveal answers.

After you create the room you'll move to the live host view (lobby → audio + exam → reveal → results).

📖 Reading Exam · Setup

You'll see the passage + live answer key on your screen — students see only the questions on their phones. They can change answers until they tap Submit. After submitting, they see only their raw score (no breakdown) until you press Reveal answers.

After you create the room you'll move to the live host view (lobby → exam → reveal → results).

🕵️ Trust No One · Setup

Some students will secretly be impostors. Crewmates win by voting them off; impostors win by outnumbering crewmates. Designed for classes of 5–25.

After you create the room you'll move to the live host view (lobby → liftoff → game → results).
