This is the AI version of the panel you use to correct papers. It marks up a student's essay the same way you do by hand with the comment bank: the same rubric, the same style of comments and highlights, and no scores. The only difference is that the AI drafts the marking for you through the API, at the depth you choose (Light / Medium / Deep).
