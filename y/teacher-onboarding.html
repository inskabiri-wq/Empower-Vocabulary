Step one

Signing in

1
Open the appGo to the Empower Lab web address in any browser, or install it as an app.
2
Sign inUse your FSM email and password, or "Continue with Google".
3
You land on the dashboardThe teacher view loads automatically for teacher accounts.

Forgot your password? Use "Forgot password?" on the sign-in screen, or ask the admin to reset it. Your account role decides what you see; the admin controls stay with the program admin.

The big picture

One place for the whole prep program

👀

See

Live progress for every student and class: sessions, scores, words learned, who is falling behind.

🎯

Set

Assign Grammar, Reading, Listening, Writing and Vocabulary work to a class, level, module or individual.

🎓

Teach

Run live classroom games, deliver the policy course, and issue certificates.

Everything is web based. Students use the same Empower Lab on phones or laptops; you use this dashboard.

  • Activity - the live feed of what students are doing

Each alert is clickable: it opens the list so you can act right away. Below the alerts you get the level distribution and a recent-activity teaser.

Students tab

Click any student to open their file

  • Quick stats - sessions, average score, words, last active
  • Per-skill breakdown - sessions, average and best per skill
  • Assignments - status and best score, with answers
  • Recent sessions - the last attempts, newest first
  • Answer review - every question, their answer vs correct

Filter the roster by class, level or module, or search by name. The drill-down is read-only, so it is safe to open on a projector during a one-to-one.

The Print / Save as PDF report button in the drill-down is admin only. It now includes the full per-question answer review, ready to share with a student or parent.

Assignments tab

Create graded work in four steps

1
Pick the skillGrammar, Reading, Listening, Writing or Vocabulary - each has its own simple form.
2
Choose the contentSelect the exam or grammar topics, and for grammar set how many questions.
3
Target who gets itA whole class, a level, a module, or named individuals.
4
Set a deadlineIt appears on the student hub and on your deadline calendar.

Reading and Listening assignments open the exact assigned exam for the student in one tap, even if that exam is hidden from free practice.

🎬

Lessons with media

Each lesson can carry an image and a short video to anchor the topic.

Classroom mode

Run the room from the projector

  • Live games - Heist, Trust No One, Race, plus Reading and Listening exams
  • Projector-safe - the board hides answers until you reveal them
  • Mixed questions - vocabulary and grammar in the same game
  • Students join - with a share code on their own devices

Open Classroom from the sidebar, pick an activity, and project the host screen. Students play along live and you control the pace.

Widgets

The classroom toolbox

The 🧰 Widgets item sits in the sidebar. Click it and a small floating tray opens.

First widget: the Class Timer.

Countdown presetsStopwatchProject modeSound + flash at zero
  • Countdown - 1, 3, 5, 10, 15 minutes, or plus / minus
  • Project mode - a full-screen clock for the board
  • Draggable - move it so it never covers your work
  • More to come - dice, name picker and others slot in here

Putting it together

A typical teaching day

1
Open OverviewScan the three alert cards. Click into anyone who needs attention.
2
Set the workCreate today's assignment and target your class with a deadline.
3
TeachRun a classroom game, use the Class Timer for tasks.
4
Check progressOpen a student's file to review answers and best scores.
5
ReportAdmins export a full PDF report for a student when needed.
